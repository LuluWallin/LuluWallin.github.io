About Me

A bit about my exciting life! I graduated high school at the age of 16 with ambitions of being a professional dancer with a degree in mathematics. However, my “zest” for life led me down a winding road in and out of college, through a stint of aesthetics and spit me out as a single mother of 2 children. Unwilling to let my self-imposed restrictions actually restrict me, I pushed through and eventually finished college with a Master’s degree. Through the whirlwind of single-motherhood, I landed in tech sales. I am now a mother of 3, married and have a handful of animals I kindly refer to as “my farm.” A problem solver at my core with a tenacious spirit , I move through through life with love, compassion, empathy and grit – always trying to help those around me.
